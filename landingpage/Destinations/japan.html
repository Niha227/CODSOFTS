Popular Locations in Japan

Tokyo

Tokyo is a bustling metropolis, offering futuristic technology, cultural landmarks like the Meiji Shrine, and world-class shopping.

Kyoto

Kyoto is known for its traditional temples, tea houses, and beautiful gardens. Don’t miss the Fushimi Inari Shrine with its iconic red torii gates.

Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji is Japan’s tallest peak and an active volcano. Climbing Fuji or viewing it from afar is an unforgettable experience.
