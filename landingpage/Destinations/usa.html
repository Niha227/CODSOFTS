Popular Locations in the USA

New York City, New York

New York City is famous for its skyline, Times Square, Broadway shows, and cultural landmarks like the Statue of Liberty and Central Park.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

The Grand Canyon is one of the most spectacular natural wonders in the world, offering breathtaking views and adventure activities like hiking and rafting.

Hollywood, Los Angeles

Hollywood is the entertainment capital of the world, known for its film industry, the Walk of Fame, and nearby attractions like Santa Monica Pier.
