Popular Locations in the Maldives

Malé - The Capital City

Malé is the capital of the Maldives and a bustling hub with colorful buildings, local markets, and beautiful mosques. Explore the lively streets or visit attractions like the Hukuru Miskiy (Friday Mosque) and the Maldives Fish Market.

Maafushi Island

Maafushi is one of the most popular islands for tourists. It offers a balance between luxury and budget options. Here you can enjoy water sports, snorkeling, and relax on pristine beaches.

Villingili Resort

Villingili Resort offers an experience of ultimate luxury. With private villas, overwater bungalows, and stunning views, it's a dream destination for honeymooners and luxury travelers.

Banana Reef

Banana Reef is one of the best diving spots in the Maldives. Known for its coral formations and colorful marine life, it is a paradise for divers and snorkelers alike.
