Popular Locations in France

Eiffel Tower, Paris

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in the world, offering stunning views of Paris from its observation decks.

Louvre Museum, Paris

The Louvre Museum is home to some of the most famous art collections, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

French Riviera

The French Riviera is known for its stunning Mediterranean coastline, luxurious resorts, and glamorous festivals.
