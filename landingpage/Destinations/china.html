Popular Locations in China

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is one of the most famous landmarks in the world, stretching over 13,000 miles. It's a must-see historical marvel that offers stunning views and rich history.

Forbidden City, Beijing

The Forbidden City is a sprawling palace complex in Beijing that once served as the imperial palace for Chinese emperors. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site and a symbol of China's rich history and culture.

Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

Known for its towering sandstone pillars, Zhangjiajie National Forest Park inspired the floating mountains in the movie *Avatar*. It's a surreal and breathtaking destination for nature lovers and adventurers.

Shanghai

Shanghai is China's largest city and a global financial hub. Its skyline is dominated by futuristic skyscrapers, including the iconic Oriental Pearl Tower, while its streets are filled with historical charm and modern culture.
